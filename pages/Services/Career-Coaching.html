Career Coaching

Career coaching helps individuals reach their goals. The process focuses on identifying what the coachees want and need from their career. Decisions are made to enable needed actions to be taken in order to accomplish their career objectives and a balance between career and personal life.

• Identification by coach of the existing undesirable performance related behaviour;
• The coachee explains where they believe the undesirable performance behaviour is based;,
• Explanation by the coach on the expectations from the coachee;
• Agreement reached on the effect of this behaviour on the coachee and the organisation;
• Identification of desirable behaviour through a collaborative plan;
• Commitment by coachee on the next steps to deal with the issue at hand;
• Make arrangements for following meetings at regular intervals;
• Monitoring of coachee's feedback in the form of comments, instructions and suggestions;
• Provide feedback to the coachee on their progress.
