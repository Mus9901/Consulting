Career History



Li Zhou is our senior consultant in China. She has near 20 years’ experience with education.



She has a Bachelors and a Masters in English literature and linguistics and is a Senior Lecturer at a very highly regarded teacher training college in Jiangsu province, China, where she runs courses for IELTS and TOEFL as well as guiding students in their future careers. Li Zhou joins us with a wealth of experience and networks within China and thus understands our much valued Chinese customers’ needs. She serves as a bridge for Chinese students to western educational institutions at all levels.



Her primary love outside her work is her family and friends. She also loves to spend time in natural surroundings either cycling or hiking.
