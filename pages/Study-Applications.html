Advice & Applications For Further Study

Every step towards further studies, whether at Boarding School, College or University, starts with a well prepared and presented application which deals effectively with the elements and information required and is also supported by the necessary documentation.

It is also able to directly monitor your application and interact with UCAS and UK Universities to ensure your application receives all the necessary attention to increase all possibilities for a successful outcome.

Application Form Checklist

• Copy of your passport

• Copy of your Visa (if you live outside of the UK)

• Transcript of your grades

• Graduation certificate (Compulsory for most, but not all courses)

• Certificate of English proficiency, e.g. IELTS or TOEFL (Not compulsory, although you may not be able to apply to certain courses, or may need to take an additional foundation year in your study if you do not have one)

• Personal statement

• Reference letter or reference name and contact information

• CV

If you are applying for a postgraduate degree, we will require a copy of your bachelor’s degree. Similarly, those applying for a PhD must provide sufficient evidence of their Master’s degree, as well as their research proposal.

Attending a Boarding School in the UK

Focus:

When you decide that the best education is at a boarding school in the UK or other European country, you need expert guidance to identify the most appropriate boarding school and help you in the process of applying and also with other arrangements. Efixis is here to help!

Package includes:

• Consultation to establish your schooling requirements;

• Identification of appropriate UK boarding schools;

• Advise on choice of curriculum and subject options;

• Organise appointments and visits to schools;

• Advise on school contracts and financial terms and options;

• Apply for a place for your child at the chosen school;

• Review and edit any supporting documentation;

• Establish if any learning support for English language is necessary;

Studying at UK University

Efixis is a UCAS Registered Centre! As such, it enjoys unique advantages when it comes to supporting those applicants who choose to employ its services when applying to study in the UK.

• Students can make their UCAS Undergraduate and / or postgraduate applications through Efixis;


• UCAS directly supports our application submission process


• UCAS ensures we offer educated advice to our applicants


• UCAS provides training to Efixis Agents on advising and supporting our applicants


• UCAS provides training to Efixis Agents on managing our applications on the UCAS Apply Service
